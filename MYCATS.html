        Meet Keety

Keety is a four year old tabby/turkish van. She was adopted from a shelter in January 2022. Keety is VERY feisty and her pastime is hissing. She loves playing with string and does NOT like to be pet.



          Meet Mika

Mika is a six month old Scottish Fold. He is a very energetic and playful kitten. He LOVES running around with his ball. He does not know how to meow yet so he screams instead. He loves to bang on doors in the middle of the night and has learned to open them. Mika also purrs NONSTOP. We call him a motorcycle.
